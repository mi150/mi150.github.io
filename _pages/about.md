I am a PhD student in Computer Science at Nanjing University, advised by Prof. Guihai Chen and Assoc. Prof. Haipeng Dai. I am also a Research Intern at the Institute for AI Industry Research (AIR), Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Yunxin Liu. Before that, I received my M.S. in Computer Science from Nanjing University and my B.S. in Software Engineering from Shandong University.

My current research interests include video analytics, LLM serving systems, and embodied AI. More broadly, I am interested in systems for large multimodal models and high-performance intelligent applications.

My research has been published in top-tier venues including NSDI, EuroSys, and INFOCOM, and journals including IEEE/ACM Transactions on Networking and IJCV.
